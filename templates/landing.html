What is Queerevent?

Queerevent is a hub for promoting and finding LGBT+ events.

At Queerevent you can:

  • Discover and promote online or physical events
  • Post and find recurring events like podcasts, Youtube channels, etc. hosted by queer people
  • Find LGBT+ events in your city or the city you are visiting
  • Add events to your calendar so you don't miss on anything
